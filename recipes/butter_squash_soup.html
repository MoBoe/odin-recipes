Butternut Squash Soup

Thick and rich butternut squash soup with tons of flavor. Super easy and quick.

Butternut Squash Soup Ingredients

  • Butter
  • Butternut squash
  • Potatoes, Onion, Carrots and Celery
  • Chicken stock
  • Salt and pepper

Directions

  1. Melt butter in a large pot over medium heat, and cook onion, celery, carrot, potatoes, and squash until lightly browned, about 5 minutes.
  2. Pour in enough of the chicken stock to cover vegetables
  3. Bring to a boil over medium-high heat.
  4. Reduce heat to low, cover pot, and simmer until all vegetables are tender, about 40 minutes.
  5. Transfer the soup to a blender, and blend until smooth.
  6. Return to the pot, and mix in any remaining stock to reach desired consistency.
  7. Season with salt and pepper.
